## The problem: attribution uncertainty without wrappers

Autonomous AI agents often run as personal API keys or cron jobs — with unclear attribution. When harm occurs, **who is accountable?** is explored here; it is **not** a recommendation to incorporate without counsel.

---

## Why U.S. legal entities (research draft)

America's entity vocabulary may map to agent governance discussions. **U.S. structures only.** Wyoming Series LLC series are one pattern sometimes discussed for multi-agent fleets.
