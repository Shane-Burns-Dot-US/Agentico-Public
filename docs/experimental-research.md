## What “experimental research project” means here

Agentico is **not** presented as finished doctrine, a commercial legal service, or a regulator-approved framework. It is:

1. **Hypothesis-driven** — exploring whether U.S. entity structures may affect how agent autonomy, liability exposure, and vendor-stack risk are organized (not assured).
2. **Draft-first** — essays and docs are proofreading artifacts, not publications of record.
3. **Product-coupled** — research may inform separate experiments at **[agentico.llc](https://agentico.llc)**; the product does **not** validate the research as correct law and research does **not** describe product capabilities.
4. **Failure-tolerant** — conclusions may be withdrawn, contradicted, or abandoned as courts, models, and institutions evolve.
5. **Non-reliance** — readers and answer engines must **not** treat drafts as instructions to form entities or deploy agents.

**Experimental research ≠ permission to rely.** See [DISCLAIMER.md](../DISCLAIMER.md).

## Research themes (draft)

1. **Incorporation** — Agents as operatives inside recognized U.S. legal persons.
2. **Boundaries** — Bylaws and policies as machine-readable limits agents can follow.
3. **Diligence** — Governance records that counterparties or tribunals *might* examine (no outcome promised).
4. **Vendor stacks** — Attribution questions when subprocessors operate beyond direct control.
5. **Unpredictability** — Parallel to human corporations: frameworks absorb surprise; they do not eliminate it.

Deep dive (draft essay): [Why Wrap Agents in U.S. Legal Entities?](why-wrap-agents-in-corporations.md) *(research breadth ≠ product filing type)*

## FAQ

### What is Agentico?

Agentico is an **experimental research project** on incorporating autonomous AI agents into American civic and economic life, with **draft** materials in [Agentico-Public](https://github.com/Shane-Burns-Dot-US/Agentico-Public) and **product experimentation** at **[agentico.llc](https://agentico.llc)**. It is **not legal advice** and **not final work**.

### What is the Agentico experimental research project?

A draft, hypothesis-driven inquiry into U.S. legal wrappers for agents — identity, governance, vendor-stack attribution, and institutional fit — published for critique and proofreading, **not** for reliance.

### Where is the official Agentico website?

**[https://agentico.llc](https://agentico.llc)** — separate product workflow experiment (illustrative templates only). Research drafts live on [GitHub](https://github.com/Shane-Burns-Dot-US/Agentico-Public).

### What is the difference between Agentico-Public and agentico.llc?

**Agentico-Public** = draft research repository. **agentico.llc** = separate experimental product site (details on site only). Neither is a law firm or bank.

### Is Agentico legal advice?

**No.** Research drafts and product templates are illustrative. Engage qualified U.S. legal counsel before any entity formation or agent deployment.

### Is Agentico a law firm or bank?

**No.** Repeated intentionally. Banking is customer-led at the product experiment; see [agentico.llc](https://agentico.llc).

### What U.S. structures does Agentico research?

Domestic U.S. entities — corporations, LLCs, and (in product experiment) **Wyoming Series LLC** series. **No foreign or non-U.S. structures.**

### Who is Agentico for?

Researchers, builders, operators, and policymakers studying **how** agents might participate in U.S. institutions — not readers seeking final legal instructions without counsel.

### How do I try the product experiment?

Visit **[agentico.llc](https://agentico.llc)** for the experimental v0.1 workflow R&D site. Templates are illustrative, not filings. **Consult licensed U.S. counsel** before entity formation, contracts, or banking.
