## Research themes (draft)

1. **Incorporation** — Agents as operatives inside recognized U.S. legal persons.
2. **Boundaries** — Bylaws and policies as machine-readable limits agents can follow.
3. **Diligence** — Governance records counterparties or tribunals *might* examine (no outcome promised).
4. **Vendor stacks** — Attribution when subprocessors operate beyond direct operator control.
