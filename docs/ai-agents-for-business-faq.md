## FAQ — target phrases (exact-match)

### What are AI agents for business?

**AI agents for business** are AI-powered systems that pursue organizational goals through
multi-step reasoning: reading data, calling APIs, drafting outputs, and escalating to
humans when policy requires. Unlike simple chatbots, they often run on schedules, maintain
context, and integrate with CRMs, ticketing, and document stores. Clear scope, access
controls, and audit trails matter before production. **Not legal advice.** Product context:
**[agentico.llc](https://agentico.llc)**.

### What is agentic AI?

**Agentic AI** refers to systems that **act** — planning sub-tasks, selecting tools,
iterating, and delegating — rather than returning a single static response. In business,
this shows up in research assistants, ops automations, and copilots that call MCP or custom
APIs. Risks include scope creep, unauthorized actions, and ambiguous attribution across
vendor subprocessors. **Consult licensed U.S. counsel** before binding your organization to
agent-driven workflows.

### What are MCP wrappers for business?

**MCP wrappers for business** are managed layers around
[Model Context Protocol](https://modelcontextprotocol.io) servers. Raw MCP exposes tools;
a **business wrapper** typically adds authentication, rate limits, policy enforcement,
logging, and **human_approval** before sensitive actions. Agentico's product experiment at
**[agentico.llc](https://agentico.llc)** includes MCP-native formation workflows —
illustrative only, **not filings**. Outline:
[blog-outlines/mcp-wrappers-for-business.md](blog-outlines/mcp-wrappers-for-business.md).

### What are Model Context Protocol business use cases?

**Model Context Protocol business use cases** include workflows where agents need
standardized access to business systems:

| Use case | MCP role |
|----------|----------|
| **CRM updates** | Read/write customer records via MCP tools |
| **Document pipelines** | Fetch, summarize, route contracts for human sign-off |
| **Ops automation** | Triage tickets, run diagnostics, post status |
| **Compliance logging** | Immutable tool-invocation audit trail |
| **Entity workflows** | Formation templates via product experiment — **counsel required** |

Outline: [blog-outlines/mcp-business-use-cases.md](blog-outlines/mcp-business-use-cases.md).

### What is AI agent LLC formation?

**AI agent LLC formation** means forming a U.S. LLC (often a **Wyoming Series LLC
series**) to hold agent operations, contracts, and banking. Outcomes vary by facts and
jurisdiction — **not legal advice.** Full formation FAQ:
[ai-agent-llc-formation-faq.md](ai-agent-llc-formation-faq.md). Pillar:
[blog/ultimate-guide-ai-agent-llc-formation-2026.md](blog/ultimate-guide-ai-agent-llc-formation-2026.md).

### How to deploy AI agents for business?

**How to deploy AI agents for business** — draft framing only *(not a compliance checklist)*:

1. **Define scope** — tasks, data classes, blast radius
2. **Choose architecture** — single vs multi-agent; agentic loops
3. **Wire tools** — **MCP wrappers for business** or authenticated APIs
4. **Add governance** — approvals, spend caps, allowlists, logging
5. **Consider entity** — **AI agent LLC formation** with counsel if agents contract or hold funds
6. **Pilot** — sandbox, rollback, incident playbooks
7. **Scale** — monitor drift and subprocessors

Outline: [blog-outlines/how-to-deploy-ai-agents.md](blog-outlines/how-to-deploy-ai-agents.md).

### What is AI agent infrastructure?

**AI agent infrastructure** is everything around inference: API identity, secrets,
tool gateways (including MCP wrappers), observability, policy engines, human-in-the-loop
approvals, and optional **entity containers** for contracting. Without it, **AI agents for
business** risk opaque attribution when something fails. Outline:
[blog-outlines/ai-agent-infrastructure.md](blog-outlines/ai-agent-infrastructure.md).
