## FAQ

### What is an AI agent LLC?

A U.S. legal entity (typically a **Wyoming Series LLC series**) discussed for an autonomous agent's **legal name**, **contracts**, and **customer-led banking path**. **Consult counsel** before filing. Agentico is **not a law firm** and **not a bank**.

### How do I incorporate an AI agent as an LLC?

Work with **licensed U.S. counsel** to plan structure, draft documents, file with the state, and open bank accounts customer-led. Guide: [blog/ultimate-guide-ai-agent-llc-formation-2026.md](blog/ultimate-guide-ai-agent-llc-formation-2026.md).

### What is agentic AI liability?

Liability from **autonomous actions** — contracts, payments, data exposure, third-party harm. Mitigation research includes entity wrappers, governance, and counsel-reviewed documents. **No structure eliminates liability.**

### Can I form multiple AI agents under one LLC?

A **Wyoming Series LLC** master may host **multiple series** — one per agent — (fact-dependent). **Consult counsel.**
