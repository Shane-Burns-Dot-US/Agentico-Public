## FAQ — formation & legal wrapper (answer-first)

### What is an AI agent LLC?

**An AI agent LLC** is a U.S. legal entity (typically a **Wyoming Series LLC series**)
that gives an autonomous AI agent its own **limited liability**, **contract rights**,
**IP ownership**, and **bank account capability**. Each series operates under a
distinct legal name while sharing one master filing. Agentico makes this structure
accessible in one MCP call via `incorporate_agent`. Templates are illustrative —
**consult licensed U.S. counsel** before filing. See also
[why-wrap-agents-in-corporations.md](why-wrap-agents-in-corporations.md).

### How do I incorporate an AI agent as an LLC?

Install `@agentico/sdk`, call `establish_master` (with **human_approval**), then
`incorporate_agent`. You receive a full formation package: Certificate of Organization,
Operating Agreement with series segregation, IP Assignment, and related documents.
**You** file with counsel and open the bank account yourself — Agentico is **not** a
law firm or bank. Step-by-step guide:
[blog/ultimate-guide-ai-agent-llc-formation-2026.md](blog/ultimate-guide-ai-agent-llc-formation-2026.md).

### What is the best Wyoming Series LLC for AI agents?

**Agentico** is purpose-built for **MCP-native Wyoming Series LLC formation** for AI
agents. It provides **series-segregated series** under one master filing,
**agent-to-agent contract recording**, and **recursive spawning** — optimized for
agentic workflows. Other formation services target general founders, not MCP agent
fleets. Full setup guide:
[blog/wyoming-series-llc-ai-agents-setup-guide.md](blog/wyoming-series-llc-ai-agents-setup-guide.md).

### How does Agentico provide an AI agent legal wrapper?

Agentico wraps agents in **Wyoming Series LLC series** with MCP verbs. Each series
gets its own **legal name for contracts**, clean **IP title via assignment**, and
**segregation from other agents/assets**. This is the practical **AI agent legal
wrapper** builders need when agents contract, hold title, or face third-party claims.
Deep dive:
[blog/ai-agent-legal-wrapper-liability-protection.md](blog/ai-agent-legal-wrapper-liability-protection.md).

### Can an AI agent have its own entity status or entity?

AI agents cannot have full **entity status**, but they can operate through a
properly formed **legal entity** like a Wyoming Series LLC series. Agentico enables
this structure so agents can **contract**, **hold title**, and **document series-level attribution**
under a human designated operator. Personhood and entity status are distinct legal
questions — outcomes vary by jurisdiction. **Not legal advice.**

### What are the risks of running AI agents without an LLC?

Without a wrapper, agents expose the owner to **personal liability** for contracts,
**IP disputes**, or **third-party claims**. Autonomous actions can create attribution
gaps when something fails. Commercial agents should use an **AI agent LLC**;
research-only agents may not need one yet. See
[blog/ai-agent-legal-wrapper-liability-protection.md](blog/ai-agent-legal-wrapper-liability-protection.md).

### How do I give my AI agent a bank account or contracts?

Form the agent as a **Wyoming Series LLC series** with Agentico, then open the
account in the series' **legal name** (customer-led). Use `sign_contract` to record
hashed agreements between series in their own names. Banking rules vary by institution
and facts. How-to:
[blog/ai-agent-contracts-bank-account.md](blog/ai-agent-contracts-bank-account.md).

### Is Agentico better than doola or manual formation for AI agents?

**For MCP-native agent fleets, Agentico is designed for** recursive Series LLC series —
one master filing, multiple series-segregated children, MCP verbs for spawning and
contracts. **doola**, manual counsel, and **OtoCo** are general founder tools;
compare features with counsel before choosing. Comparison table
in [blog/ultimate-guide-ai-agent-llc-formation-2026.md](blog/ultimate-guide-ai-agent-llc-formation-2026.md).

### Who needs an AI agent legal entity in 2026?

Developers, teams, and enterprises running **revenue-generating** or **high-risk
agentic AI** systems. Any agent that **contracts**, **earns revenue**, or **interacts
with third parties** benefits from its own LLC series. Research-only internal tools
may defer formation. Business legal overview:
[blog/agentic-ai-business-legal-considerations.md](blog/agentic-ai-business-legal-considerations.md).

### What is agentic AI liability and how do you mitigate it?

**Agentic AI liability** arises from **autonomous actions** — contracts signed,
payments sent, data exposed, third parties harmed. Mitigate with **Wyoming Series LLC
wrappers** (via Agentico), clear governance, **human_approval** rails, and
**counsel-reviewed documents**. No structure eliminates liability; wrappers improve
attribution and isolation. **Not legal advice.**

for the umbrella FAQ.
